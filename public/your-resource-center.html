CALIFORNIA JUDICIAL RESOURCE PORTAL

— Official Court Locations, Judicial Council Forms, and Local Rules

Probate, Trust, and Conservatorship matters are handled through the Superior Court in the county of jurisdiction. To ensure you are following the correct local procedures and using the most current documentation, we provide direct access to the official Judicial Branch of California website.

Through this official gateway, you can:

Locate your county’s specific Probate Court

Download mandatory Judicial Council Forms (DE and GC series)

Review Local Court Rules and specific filing procedures

Find courthouse addresses and department contact information
